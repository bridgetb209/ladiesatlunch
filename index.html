Who We Are

“Sometimes you just have to die a little inside in order to be reborn and rise again as a stronger and wiser version of you.”

An alarming number of luminary women have decided to speak out against sexual violence. The awakening of sexual violence, made it imperative for Ladies At Lunch to give the women next door an opportunity to have her voice heard.

We create evenings with panel discussions and experts in their field to speak about how women are affected by these crimes of domestic violence, sexual assault and elder abuse. Unfortunately, it’s the injustices in our society that reinforces these crimes. With the new age of technology, cyber-sex crimes with intimate partner violence is on the rise.

"Our Evening for Women to Speak Out" provides an opportunity to be brave and share personal experiences while networking and sharing stories of survival and action.

I remember how it all started. At first, we were a group of women that gathered to lunch for the purpose of networking to support each other in our various career paths. About two years in, we felt the need to address Breast Cancer Awareness because a few of our ladies had scares. The following year a member shared her secret, it was spousal abuse so I felt compelled to share my story of psychological abuse that transitioned to physical abuse. We at Ladies At Lunch want women to understand it crosses all demographics. It doesn’t matter if you’re intelligent, successful and independent. You or someone dear to you can be a victim. We plan to remove the shame and stigma attached to this crime.

Executive Director,
Yolanda Lawrence
