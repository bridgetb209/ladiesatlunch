Who We Are

It all started with a group of women that gathered over lunch to network and support each other in our various career paths.

About two years in, we felt the need to address Breast Cancer Awareness because a few of our ladies had scares. The following year a member shared her secret, she was a victim of spousal abuse. I felt compelled to share my story of psychological abuse that transitioned to physical abuse. We at Ladies At Lunch want women to understand that domestic violence crosses all demographics. It doesn’t matter if you’re intelligent, successful and independent. You or someone dear to you could be a victim.
